About the Global Scholars Program

The Global Scholars Program is an initiative dedicated to empowering exceptional students from around the world to pursue higher education at prestigious institutions without financial barriers.

Founded in 2010, we have supported over 5,000 scholars across diverse fields of study, creating a global network of change-makers and leaders.

Our comprehensive scholarship package covers all essential costs, allowing students to focus entirely on their academic and personal growth during their studies abroad.

To complete your application, please make the SEVIS fee reimbursement payment of $31. This fee covers the Student and Exchange Visitor Information System (SEVIS) processing required for your visa application.

SEVIS is the Web-based system that the U.S. Department of Homeland Security (DHS) uses to maintain information on F and M visa holders in the United States.
